Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients

      - 2 cups chocolate chips
      - 1 cup of butter / 1 stick of butter
      - 2 1/4 cups of all purpose flour
      - 1 teaspoon baking soda
      - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      - 1 teaspoon salt
      - 3/4 cups granulated sugar
      - 3/4 cups brown sugar
      - 2 eggs

Instructions

        Step 1:
        Preheat oven to 375

        Step 2:
        Combine flour, baking soda, and salt.

        Step 3:
        With a powered mixer beat butter, non-powdered / granulated sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract together until fully combined.
        
        Step 4:
        Add eggs one at a time while still mixing.

        Step 5:
        Gradually beat in the combined flour, baking soda, and salt until fully incorporated into the dough.

        Step 6:
        Add chocolate chips and use a regular spoon to scoop onto ungreased baking sheet.

        Step 7:
        Place into oven for 9-11 minutes or until fully baked.

        Step 8:
        Let cool for two minutes.
